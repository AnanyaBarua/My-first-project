Why choose us?

Welcome to the plant deck at the Botanical Garden. We are a commercial nursery located within the botanical garden; 100% of our proceeds go towards the garden’s operating expenses. When you buy a plant from us, you are directly supporting the Botanical Garden.

Visit the plant deck and see what we have to offer. Whether you’re landscaping your yard, looking for succulent dish garden gifts, or searching for that rare greenhouse addition, you’re sure to find it on the plant deck.

Currently, due to the 2020 Coronavirus pandemic, the Gardens are open with restrictions on gatherings, including our popular plant sales. However, our plants continue to flourish, so we are pleased to offer our Online Plant Sale.
